Definitions for signs that may be new:
  • Drooping of Nasolabial Fold: These are the 'smile lines', that run from the nose to the corner of the mouth. Think of it as looking for relaxed facial muscle tone. See page 10 of [2] for an example. Googling will mostly bring up its relation to cosmetic surgery.
  • Mandibular Breathing: See this brief video for a good overview.
  • Vocal Cord Grunting: "A sound produced predominantly on expiration, related to vibrations of the vocal cords."
  • Decreased Response to Visual Stimuli: No eye tracking movement.
  • Decreased Response to Verbal Stimuli: No verbal communication (noises don't count), unable to follow commands.
  • Unable to close eyelids: Unable to close when asked, eyes open without observed blinking.
  • The other signs are either self-explanatory or can be easily searched for online.
